Frequently Asked Questions
What is Model United Nations?
Students can learn about diplomacy, international relations, and the UN through the academic activity and educational simulation known as Model United Nations. Delegates, or participants in Model United Nations conferences, are assigned to a committee and a nation to represent throughout the conference.
What is the dress code?
Western business attire for both female and male participants.
What is the official language?
The official language is English.
