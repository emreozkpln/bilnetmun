Letter from Secretary-General
Dear Participants,
As the Secretary General of the first Bilnet Schools Model United Nations, it is my great pleasure to welcome you all to our conference. As the BILMUN team we worked extremely hard to present you a magnificent experience over the next three days. I have participated various conferences and started to think that why our school doesn't organize these conferences. Since that time we have been working really hard to organize a wondrous conference. If our academic and organization team hadn't worked that hard, we wouldn't have been able to fulfill our goal. Thanks to them we had the opportunity to come together in this conference and we are really proud to introduce our first but hopefully annual conference BILMUN. Over the next few days you will face various problems that our world has to overcome in our everyday lives. I recommend you to associate with your fellow delegates and try to find effective solutions by attending debates in a regardful way. I look forward to meet you all and see your solution ideas related to the topics you're going to discuss in these few days.
Sincerest regards, Hilal Öztürk Secretary General
Letter from Director-General
Dear Participants,
First of all, I would like to welcome you all. As Director General, I am very happy and excited to see you among us. As BILMUN, we have thought of you, our valuable participants, in every step we have taken on this path we have started. Thanks to the experience of our academic team, the subject depth of our committees and the opportunities provided by BİLNET schools, we endeavour to offer you an unforgettable conference experience. We aim to contribute to your academic life, social skills, problem solving and analytical thinking skills. I look forward to seeing you one-on-one. Welcome to BILMUN!
Eren Özbakır, Director General
