Vision & Mission
Vision
Now is the moment for fresh ideas and analytical thinking skills to develop solutions to universal challenges. Our world, which is always growing and changing at an unpredictable rate, has brought with it unavoidable issues as a result of this expansion and development. The challenges caused by this rapid growth have disproportionately impacted Generation Z. ones who are most affected by problems are always ones who seek solutions. And we can find these solutions by thinking creatively, peacefully, and analytically. As BILMUN'24, we are back to discuss potential solutions to existing challenges!
Mission
Our objective as BILMUN'24 is to create a conference that is peaceful, competitive, innovative, sustainable, and solution-oriented. We believe that by publishing these articles, we will help our participants improve their educational, social, and analytical skills. Our primary responsibility is to encourage new and innovative ideas. A few expected elements will assist us in carrying out our task. These are the following:
A COMPETITIVE BUT PEACEFUL DISCUSSION ENVIRONMENT:
Since we will be welcoming our participants in a diplomatic environment, the discussion will be passionate. There may be some disagreement during this conversation due to variables such as the perspectives of the countries (or people) represented by our participants, their political stances, and similar factors. One of our primary responsibilities as the BILMUN'24 team is to transform all debates into a solution-oriented discussion atmosphere. As previously said, the goal of this healthy dialogue is to develop our members ' academic and social skills.
WE TRUST YOUTH AND THEIR FRESH IDEAS !
It is extremely clear what kinds of issues our participants come from and represent. Pandemics, economic downturns, minor military confrontations, and diplomatic crises are all possibilities. Despite their youth, they have a lot of political conclusions because of the current state of world politics. And these concepts are both novel and pleasant. We aim to listen to our young people ' s ideas, formulate the solutions and policies they will implement, and improve their social skills. Why shouldn 't a new leader arise from among our ranks?
CREATING AN UNFORGETTABLE EXPERIENCE AND UNFORGATTABLE FRIENDSHIP
Since communication is the basis of our conference, our participants have to remain in touch with each other. During their three days together, they will develop great friendships, build a wonderful network, and have an unforgettable experience.
BY SHARING YOUNG MINDS , WE MAY BRING ATTENTION TO WORLDWIDE PROBLEMS AND FOSTER A BROADER MENTALITY !
As previously stated, the majority of our participants are young people from Generation Z. We can be informed about the majority of global crises thanks to technology, and given that we live in the age of technology, Generation Z has a high level of knowledge about the crises that have occurred. Our objective is to assist them in developing a larger mentality by sharing the knowledge they have with others.
Our most essential responsibility is to ensure that all of our participants enjoy a positive conference experience. The procedures that need to be followed during the conference are identical to those used by the United Nations. Our participants will have an almost life-like experience and will strengthen their political and diplomatic thinking skills as a result of these procedures. We hope to improve our participants' academic lives, social skills, thoughts, and foreign languages. We as BILMUN'24 are ready and waiting for you!
